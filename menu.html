Menu

The food that we offer is an amalgamation of various regional dishes that are very popular in different parts of India. The dishes are freshly made to order from fresh ingredients and differ from the way traditional Indian restaurants cook their food. Our menu is healthy, less influenced by heavily flavored spices but is filled with the authentic flavours of India.
